About David

Want to know more about me?

I'm a software developer, currently living in Columbus, Ohio. I've been working for a small company for the last four years, after interning at Lexis Nexis during college.

I graduated from Wright State University in 2013, with a BS in Computer Science. While in college, I enjoyed embedded programming and mobile development, although I have not had the opportunity to use either at my current job. I also had the opportunity to learn some German. While I have not yet had a real chance to use it, I look forward to being able to travel to Germany some day to confuse some native speakers with my gibberish.

The business I currently work for is, Vivonet. When I started, it was a local company called "Syrus Restaurant Information Services", but in late 2015, we were acquired by Vivonet Acquisitions. As Syrus, we produced behind the scenes reporting tools for the quick service industry. Vivonet's main product is a point of sale. We are working to slowly integrate the Syrus product with the Vivonet POS.
